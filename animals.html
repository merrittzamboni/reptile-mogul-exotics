Invertebrates

We have a wide variety of invertebrates available. Everything from tarantulas to scorpions, along with supplements, food, and everything you need to get your habitat set up properly.

We will only sell dangerous animals to those who have experience and are responsible enough to own and take care of such things!

Special Orders

For special orders, call or email us with your name, phone number, and what you are looking to order. For emails, in the subject bar put the name of the species or product you are looking to get. A deposit may be needed depending on the price of the animal/product and how hard it is to get. Deposits are anywhere from 20% to 50%, depending on price and rarity. You will be added to a list with others waiting for special orders, and we will contact you with price and information on whether or not we can purchase the item.

Also, call for information on possible payment plans! If an agreement on a payment plan is reached, the item cannot be picked up until it is paid off.
