Supplies

Products

We have all the supplies and dry goods you are looking for to keep a happy pet! We stock:

  • Bulbs: (Heat/UV/Fluorescent)
  • Hides: (Rock/Plastic/Bark)
  • Bedding: Aspen, Cypress, Bark, Rock, Rabbit Pellets
  • Climbs: Branches, Vines, etc.
  • Heat: Bulbs, Cable, Tape, Mats, Thermostats, Thermometers
  • Dishes: Water, Food, Cricket Hides
  • Handling: Hooks, Tongs
  • Husbandry: Probes, Tubes, Breeding Boxes
  • Food: Dry & Canned, Supplements

...and much more! And if we don't have it in stock, we can order it for you! We are partnered with Zoo Med and Exo-Terra, and we can order anything from their catalogs at prices that can't be beat!

Feeders

We have feeders with the best prices in town. We breed our own rodents and have a big supply of rats, mice, and even African soft fur (ASF) rats in all sizes. Rodents are available live or frozen, and we can humanely pre-kill the rodents if needed. Our feeders are given only the highest quality food, ensuring that your pet gets only the best!

We stock everything you will need to keep your pet nourished, including:

  • Crickets
  • Superworms
  • Mice
  • Rats
  • African Soft Furred Rats
  • Dry & Canned Foods
  • Supplements
