Visit the Sepentarium

Reptile Mogul proudly hosts a serpentarium with a large collection of reptiles, including venomous snakes! For a small admission fee, visitors can browse our collection of reptiles from all over the world. Currently we are featuring our North American Rattlesnake display. The serpentarium is fun for all ages!

Inquire about hosting special events at the serpentarium, such as birthday parties. Packages include pictures with the live animals!
